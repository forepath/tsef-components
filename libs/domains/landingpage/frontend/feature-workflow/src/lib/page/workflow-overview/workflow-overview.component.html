AI-Assisted Development Workflow

TSEF's streamlined workflow enhances quality and consistency in every project. Starts with research to explore possibilities, creates a detailed plan to guide development, implements with precision, and confidently ships features.

/research

1. Research

Explores topics deeply through multiple sources and perspectives. Synthesizes findings, evaluates different approaches, and uncovers potential risks and constraints. Saves everything in a research file within .research/ with clear citations and actionable recommendations that guide the planning process.

2. Plan

Transforms research into a clear roadmap by reviewing documentation and synthesizing findings. Breaks down features into manageable, actionable tasks and identifies the right locations within the domain structure. Leverages Nx generators to streamline setup, then documents everything in .plans/ with well-defined acceptance criteria.

/plan
/implement

3. Implement

Brings plans to life by building features that align with established conventions and naming standards. Works on dedicated feature branches, commits changes with clear, focused messages, and keeps documentation up to date. Prioritizes quality by writing thorough tests and running the full validation suite before finalizing work.

4. Ship

Shares work by opening pull requests with descriptive titles and filling out PR templates completely. Engages code owners and stakeholders for review, responds thoughtfully to feedback, and ensures all checks pass successfully before merging changes.

/ship
